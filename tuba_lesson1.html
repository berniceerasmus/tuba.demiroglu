Grammar — What Are Irregular Verbs?

Verbs that don't follow the -ed rule

7 min ▼

REGULAR vs IRREGULAR

Regular verbs: just add -ed. Irregular verbs: completely different past form — no rules!

Regular: watch → watched · play → played · cook → cooked
Irregular: go → went · see → saw · eat → ate
✗ goed · seed · eated — these don't exist!
🇹🇷Düzensiz fiiller -ed almaz. Her birinin ayrı geçmiş hali var — sadece ezberlemek gerekiyor!
Good news: Once you know them, they feel completely natural. Native speakers never think about them — they just say "went" automatically. That's our goal!
