Grammar — Connectors: because, so, but, although, however

Link your ideas and sound more natural

10 min ▼

BECAUSE — reason / why

Sentence + because + reason
I stayed home because I was tired.
She didn't eat the cake because she doesn't like chocolate.
✗ Because I was tired, so I stayed home — don't use because + so together!
🇹🇷because = çünkü. Sebep/neden sorusunu cevaplar.

SO — result / consequence

Reason/cause, so + result
I was tired, so I stayed home.
The shop was closed, so we went home.
🇹🇷so = bu yüzden / bu nedenle. Sonuç bildirir.

BUT / ALTHOUGH / HOWEVER — contrast

but = simple contrast in one sentence
I love coffee but I don't drink it at night.
although = surprising contrast (= even though)
Although I was tired, I went to the gym.
however = contrast between two sentences (more formal)
I love coffee. However, I don't drink it at night.
🇹🇷but = ama (basit) · although = ...olmasına rağmen · however = ancak (iki cümle arası, daha resmi)
Quick guide: WHY? → because · RESULT? → so · CONTRAST? → but / although / however

Exercise 3 — Error Correction

Exercise 3 Fix the connector error in each sentence.
Wrong connector

Double connector

Wrong connector
She didn't like the film. So, she watched it three times.
Wrong position
She spoke really well although she was confident. (she WAS confident — no contrast)
