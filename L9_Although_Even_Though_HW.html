Homework 09

Although & Even Though

Practise contrast connectors in sentences and a paragraph

Homework 0920–25 minConnectorsSentence Structure
Part 1

Complete with although or even though

25 pts
Both "although" and "even though" show contrast. Fill in either one (both can be correct).
1it was raining, we had a great time.
2she was tired, she kept working.
3the food was expensive, it was delicious.
4I don't like mornings, I always wake up early.
5I find grammar hard, I am improving every lesson.
Part 2

Error correction — although mistakes

25 pts
Find and correct the error in each sentence.
Remove the extra word
Although she was hungry, but she waited for dinner.
Add punctuation/fix structure
Although it was late. She kept reading.
Wrong connector position
She passed the exam. Although she studied hard.
Part 3

Writing — a paragraph about your week

30 pts
Write 5–6 sentences about something that happened this week. Use "although" or "even though" at least TWICE.
